WALMART INTERNET DELIVERY SERVICE
The purpose of the analysis is to know the current status of orders made by Walmart customers on the Internet in Mexico According to the analysis we could see which days are the ones with the highest orders, in what area, in what state at what time and how far you have to travel from the store to make the delivery. In the end you can know where to open new stores to meet orders or open new stores in a market not yet used.
